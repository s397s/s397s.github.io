Handheld Toddler Safe Windmill

by Berry Bunch

Ready to create a fun and safe toy for your toddler? In this video, we’ll show you how to make a handheld windmill that’s perfect for little hands. Follow our easy steps to craft a colorful and sturdy windmill using child-friendly materials. By the end, you’ll have a delightful toy that spins in the breeze, sure to bring a smile to your little one’s face. Let’s get crafting and watch those blades twirl!

What You Will Need

Material

Color foam sheets

Paper straws

Pipe cleaner

Supplies

Scissors

Stapler / Glue dots

Ruler

Pen


Step 1

Cut the foam to 12.5 x 12.5cm sheet and fold it

(0:28)

Step 2

Cut holes

(0:50)

Step 3

Make cuts follow the fold pattern

(1:02)

Step 4

Fold all the splits to join the holes together

(1:34)

Step 5

Stick it down

(1:57)

Step 6

Cut out a small circle using left over foams, make a hole in it

(2:06)

Step 7

Wrap a pipe cleaner around a straw, pop on the circle. Feed the pipe cleaner through the back of the paper

(2:47)

Step 8

Cut off the excessive part of the pipe cleaner, wrap it around in circular mottion

(3:25)
