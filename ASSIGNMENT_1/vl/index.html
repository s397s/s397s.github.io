5) What is your impression of the intended primary goal of the interactive experience?


The core purpose of this website is to provide valuable knowledge and engaging experience about historical, cultural, and architectural aspects of the Wall Disney Concert Hall to its audience; which can be achieved by creating interactive features to encourages users to actively engage with the content rather than passively consuming it. This connects to active learning theories in academics, which suggest that people remember things better when they're actively engaged in learning process. This is a fundamental trait that most information-based websites should embody, and “Sculpting Harmony” has done such a great job. Moreover, this website gives off an impression that it might aim on a wide range of audiences based on the way the interface was designed.
